Exposure + Maintenance, Security, Fire risk → Recommendations → Grading A–E) from

## Sheet S1 — Residential Risk Survey

**Reviewer:** ______________________ **Test property/file:** ______________ **Date:** ________

### Section checklist — tick items that belong, cross what doesn't, add what's missing

**Construction**
- ☐ All elevations; roof covering as visible; wall material close-up
- ☐ Thatch/timber/non-standard elements + distance of thatch structures from dwelling
- ☐ Outbuildings; unapproved additions question; asbestos roofing note

**Occupancy**
- ☐ Owner/tenant/holiday home; unoccupancy periods (>30/60 days)
- ☐ Business use from home (stock, visitors, equipment); short-term letting

**Protection**
- ☐ Geyser installation (drip tray, valves); DB board + earth leakage
- ☐ Gas installation + CoC; fireplace/braai/flue
- ☐ Solar/inverter/battery: location, ventilation, installer certificate
- ☐ Lightning protection (esp. thatch); extinguisher/fire blanket if any

**Exposure**
- ☐ Street view from gate; neighbours/boundaries; slopes, watercourses
- ☐ Flood history; veld/bush interface; large trees over structures

**Maintenance**
- ☐ Gutters/fascias, waterproofing, boundary walls, general upkeep
- ☐ Known damp/cracks; pool fence/net (liability)

**Security**
- ☐ Perimeter (walls, electric fence + CoC), gates/motors
- ☐ External doors/locks, burglar bars, security gates
- ☐ Alarm (monitored? armed response? zones), beams, CCTV
- ☐ Safe type/mounting vs jewellery/firearms requirements
- ☐ **Policy-warranty vs reality gap check** (the key output)

**Fire risk summary** — ignition sources / detection / suppression / separation

**Recommendations & grading**
- ☐ Requirement vs Improvement categories with timelines (immediate/30/90/renewal)
- ☐ Grade A–E, surveyor judgement, NOT auto-computed

## Sheet S2 — Commercial / Property Risk Survey

**Reviewer:** ______________________ **Test property/file:** ______________ **Date:** ________

> Aimed at small-to-mid commercial risks (offices, retail, light industrial, body
> corporates). Heavy/complex industrial risks are physical-survey by default —
> confirm this boundary below.

### Section checklist

**Construction**
- ☐ Elevations; roof; fire walls/separations between units; mezzanines
- ☐ Combustible construction — esp. sandwich/insulated panels (core material?)
- ☐ Unprotected structural steel in fire-loaded areas; approved plans for alterations

**Occupancy**
- ☐ Walk-through of every occupancy area: process, storage (commodity, stacking
  height, aisles), kitchens, hazardous processes (welding, spraying, charging)
- ☐ All tenants + activities (multi-tenant); incompatible-tenant check
- ☐ Flammable/hazardous materials: types, quantities, storage method
- ☐ Stock values + seasonal peaks; hours; overnight occupancy

**Protection**
- ☐ Extinguishers/hose reels (count + service labels — SANS 1475 currency)
- ☐ Detection type/coverage/monitoring; sprinkler valves + last inspection
- ☐ Emergency exits/signage; DB boards; electrical CoC; thermographic scan history
- ☐ Generator/inverter/fuel storage arrangements

**Exposure**
- ☐ Neighbouring occupancies per boundary + separation distances; shared walls/roof voids
- ☐ Flood/stormwater history; area context

**Maintenance & housekeeping**
- ☐ Housekeeping in work/storage areas; waste skips vs building; yard; roof drainage
- ☐ Hot-work permit system; smoking policy; planned maintenance regime

**Security**
- ☐ Perimeter, access control, guarding, alarm/CCTV + retention
- ☐ Cash handling limits; high-value stock storage vs warranties; key control
- ☐ **Policy-warranty vs reality gap check**

**Fire risk summary** — fire load vs protection; biggest single-loss scenario
narrative; business-interruption vulnerability comment

**Recommendations & grading** — as residential, PLUS:
- ☐ Mandatory question: *"Is this risk adequately surveyable virtually?"* — No routes
  to physical survey recommendation
